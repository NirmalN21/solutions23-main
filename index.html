ABOUT US

We are THE TECHNICAL BOARD of Army Institute Of Technology.
Our aim is to foster creativity among students by providing them sufficient opportunities to develop both technical and co-curricular skills through tech-events.
And for our beloved techies we are back again with the 9th edition of our Technical Fest, SOLUTIONS
It's going to be a 3-day roller-coaster ride full of thrill, cut-throat competition and unforgettable memories. Here, you will experience challenges, you have never seen before.

Speaker Session

We are excited to announce our prime speaker session inviting Kushal Vijay. A popular Indian YouTuber who has gained a significant following through his entertaining and informative videos. Kushal creates content on a wide range of topics, including technology, gadgets, smartphones, and software. He is a renowned expert in this field and is expected to share valuable insights and ideas that can inspire and motivate the students to achieve their goals.

Bike Show

One of the most heart-storming events of SOLUTIONS 2K23. A variety of “super-bikes”, will be set to thrill the audience with their buzzing exhausts, wheelies, burnouts, and other stunts. BMW S1000 will remain the centre of attraction during the show, as you’ll see it racing through the campus. And few lucky ones might get a chance of getting clicked with the "SUPERBIKES" and even ride it along with the team.
